James Boadi is a voice actor that does narration,
commercials and character voices. Before transitioning into voice
acting he had a background in music as a saxophone player, and has
performed in concerts and theaters. As such, he developed an interest for
doing voice overs during one of many visits to various performance centers
and has loved it ever since.


Feel free to get in touch! Happy to take any requests.
